About Raja Lodge 🏨

Welcome to Raja Lodge Ooty, serving guests with warmth and care since 1987. With over 45 years of heritage, we are located just 2 minutes from Ooty Railway Station and Bus Stand. Nestled close to every major tourist attraction, Raja Lodge is the perfect choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience in the Queen of Hills 🌿.
