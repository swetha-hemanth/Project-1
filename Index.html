About Raja Lodge 🏨

Welcome to Raja Lodge Ooty, serving guests with warmth and care since 1987. Nestled close to every major tourist attraction, Raja Lodge is the perfect choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience in the Queen of Hills 🌿.
